01 // THE PHILOSOPHY

The Myth of the Price Tag

High fashion houses charge up to 10x the actual structural cost of a garment simply to fund corporate marketing campaigns and retail real estate. ZEVROK operates on a direct-to-consumer infrastructure. We focus 100% of our budget on textiles and cutting-edge design, cutting out middle-men.

02 // ARTIFACT ARCHITECTURE

Uncompromising Fabrication

Our drops are characterized by custom milled fabrics. Expect heavy-weight 450GSM organic loopback cotton hoodies, custom-dyed anti-shrink interlock combed cotton tees, and industrial-grade reinforced stitch patterns meant to last generations.
